About HELIXA

HELIXA AeroSystems is a research-driven UAV startup focused on self-charging drone technology using double-helix micro turbines integrated into propeller systems.

Working Principle

1. Airflow Capture

During drone flight, the airflow passing through the propeller hub enters the double-helix turbine module.

2. Turbine Rotation

The double-helix turbine converts aerodynamic energy into rotational mechanical energy.

3. Micro Generator

The rotating turbine drives a miniature generator to produce electrical power.

4. Battery Charging

Generated power is regulated and fed back into the drone battery to extend flight endurance.
